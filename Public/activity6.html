About Me

In the 9th of July, 2005, Cythel Marie was born on the place of Magsaysay, Compostela Valley, Davao de Oro. Her Mother is Mergil Rusiana,
and her father is Forunato Hidalgo Manait. When she was 2 years old, her family moved in Siay, Zamboanga Sibugay. Unfortunately,
her father died when she was 4 years old. My mother beacme a widow, taking care of Cythel and her 2 siblings namely Charmiel Manait
and Charmae Manait.

She spent her elementary days at Coloran Elementary School and she graduated in year 2017. In her highschool, she went to Siay National
High School where she developed her hobbies: drawing, reading, and watching animes. Her highschool was colorful and tough which opened
her eyes about life. She graduated high school in year 2021 with honors. She also spent her senior highschool days at Siay National High
School and graduated in year 2023 with honors. Fortunately she was granted a DOST Scholarship that can help her college, lifting her family's
financial burden.

At Western Mindanao State University main campus, she studied Bachelor of Science in Architecture. Her first year went well, she thought
that she can excell in the field of architecture, considering that she's a scholar. However on her second year in studying her course, she
can no longer see herself working in the field of architecture due to self pressure and disapointments. She also developed health concerns
which hindered her from studying her course.So, she decided to shift to Computer Science in WMSU ESU Ipil campus and reverted to Islam
because she thought that these can bring her peace and spark back. It is a tough decision to make but she pursued it. Now, she's studying in
Bachelor of Science in Computer Science with her new faith. This transition allowed her to heal and to rediscover her new passion without
the weight of her past anxieties. In this new environment, she finally found her spark back that she thought she had lost, proving that
shifting paths isn't a failure but a brave step toward self-perseverance.
