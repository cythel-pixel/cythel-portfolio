CYTHEL'S CLASSIC CREAMY MAC & CHEESE RECIPE

Materials:

  • Saucepan (for cheese sauce)
  • Strainer
  • Whisk or wooden spoon
  • Measuring cups & spoons
  • Stove
  • Knife & cutting board (if grating cheese)

Ingredients (Serves 3–4):

  • 250 g (about 2 cups) elbow macaroni
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk (warm is better)
  • 2 cups shredded cheese
    • Cheddar works best (you can mix with mozzarella or parmesan)
  • ½ teaspoon salt (adjust to taste)
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • Optional:
    • ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
    • ¼ teaspoon paprika or mustard powder (for extra flavor)

Procedure:

  1. Cook the Pasta
    1. Bring a pot of water to a boil
    2. Add a pinch of salt
    3. Cook the macaroni according to package instructions until al dente
    4. Drain and set aside
  2. Make the Cheese Sauce
    1. In a saucepan over medium heat, melt the butter
    2. Add the flour and whisk continuously for about 1 minute (this removes the raw flour taste)
    3. Slowly pour in the milk while whisking to avoid lumps
    4. Cook for 2–3 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly
    5. Lower the heat and add the cheese gradually, stirring until melted and smooth
    6. Season with salt, pepper, and optional spices.
  3. Combine
    1. Add the cooked macaroni to the cheese sauce
    2. Stir until all the pasta is well coated
    3. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed

Serve:

  • Serve hot and creamy
  • Optional toppings: extra cheese, breadcrumbs, or a sprinkle of parsley
